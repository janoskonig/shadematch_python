Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ShadeMatch ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our color vision testing and color mixing application. This research has been approved by the Semmelweis University Regional and Institutional Committee of Science and Research Ethics under approval number SE RKEB 167/2025.

Important: All data collected is anonymous and not traceable to individuals. Your participation in this research is voluntary and you may withdraw at any time.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Anonymous User Data

We collect the following anonymous data for research purposes:

  • User ID: A randomly generated 6-character identifier (not traceable to individuals)
  • Birthdate: For demographic analysis in color vision research
  • Gender: For demographic analysis in color vision research
  • Account Creation Date: Timestamp of user registration

2.2 Color Mixing Session Data

For each color mixing session, we collect:

  • Target Color: RGB values of the target color to be mixed
  • Drop Counts: Number of drops used for each color (white, black, red, yellow, blue)
  • Computed color difference: A technical color-difference score may be stored for research analysis; it is not shown to participants in the app interface.
  • Session Duration: Time taken to complete the mixing task
  • Session Timestamp: When the session was completed
  • Skip Status: Whether the session was skipped
  • Skip perception (when applicable): If you skip a trial while still mixing, we may ask you to rate how acceptable your mix was compared to the target (e.g., in a facial-prosthesis color-matching context). For analysis, this may be combined with the computed color difference into a short outcome label (e.g. perfect match vs. acceptable vs. large difference).

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect minimal technical information:

  • Session management data for application functionality
  • Cookie consent preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Research: To study color vision and color mixing performance
  • Application Functionality: To provide personalized color testing experiences
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our application
  • Improvement: To enhance our application and research methods

4. Cookie Usage

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, serve personalized content, and analyze our traffic. By clicking "Accept All", you consent to our use of cookies.

4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our application, you will see a cookie consent banner with the following message:

"We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, serve personalized content, and analyze our traffic. By clicking 'Accept All', you consent to our use of cookies."

4.2 Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • session_id: Maintains your session during color mixing activities
  • csrf_token: Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery
  • user_preferences: Stores basic application preferences

4.3 Cookie Consent Management

We use a cookie consent management system that stores:

  • cookie_consent: Your consent preferences for different cookie categories

Note: We do not use analytics, marketing, or advertising cookies. All data collection is anonymous and for research purposes only.

5. Data Sharing

Since all data collected is anonymous and not traceable to individuals, we may share anonymized research data:

  • Research Collaboration: With other researchers for scientific advancement in color vision research
  • Academic Publication: In scientific journals and conferences (data remains anonymous)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or regulatory authorities

Note: No personal information is shared as all data is collected anonymously.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the anonymous research data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Anonymous data collection (no personal identifiers stored)
  • Research data is stored separately from any identifying information

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Since all data is collected anonymously and is not traceable to individuals, traditional GDPR rights do not apply. However, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw Participation: Stop using the application at any time
  • Cookie Consent: Withdraw consent for cookie usage through our cookie consent banner
  • Information: Request information about the research and data collection practices
  • Contact: Contact us with any questions about the research

Note: Since data is anonymous, we cannot identify or delete specific user data. You may stop participating in the research at any time by simply not using the application.

8. Data Retention

We retain anonymous research data for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete the research study
  • Publish research findings
  • Comply with academic and legal obligations
  • Support future research in color vision

Note: Since data is anonymous, there is no way to identify or delete specific user data. All data is retained for legitimate research purposes only.

9. Age Requirements

This research application is intended for adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, please do not use this application.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
