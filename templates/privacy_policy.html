  • {{ t('Skip Status:') }} {{ t('Whether the session was skipped') }}
  • {{ t('Skip perception (when applicable):') }} {{ t('If you skip a trial while still mixing, we may ask you to rate how acceptable your mix was compared to the target (e.g., in a facial-prosthesis color-matching context).   • {{ t('Step-by-step mixing actions:') }} {{ t('For research on mixing strategies, we record each drop you add or remove during a mixing attempt, with timestamps, the mix state before and after each step, and the app version in use') }}
