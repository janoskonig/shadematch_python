Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ShadeMatch ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our color vision testing and color mixing application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • User ID: A randomly generated 6-character identifier
  • Birthdate: For research purposes related to color vision
  • Gender: For demographic analysis (optional)
  • Test Results: Color mixing performance data and timing

2.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Session data for application functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Research: To study color vision and color mixing performance
  • Application Functionality: To provide personalized color testing experiences
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our application
  • Improvement: To enhance our application and research methods

4. Cookie Usage

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

4.1 Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly:

  • Session management
  • User authentication
  • Security tokens

4.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand website usage (with your consent):

  • Page views and user interactions
  • Performance metrics
  • Error tracking

4.3 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings (with your consent):

  • Display preferences
  • Language settings
  • Theme preferences

Note: We do not use marketing or advertising cookies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • For research purposes with anonymized data
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure data storage
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for cookie usage

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Conduct research
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes

9. Age Requirements

Our application is intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
