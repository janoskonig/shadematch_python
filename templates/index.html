Before you start

A few quick checks so your screen shows colors faithfully.

  • Turn off Night Shift / True Tone / Night Light and any blue-light filter (in your operating system).
  • Use steady, indirect ambient lighting. Avoid direct sunlight on the screen and reflective glare.
  • Set display brightness to about 50–75%.
  • For best results, use full-screen mode.
