8) Mixing strategy archetypes (rule-based)

Archetypes are assigned per attempt from action-path behavior (not manually labeled).

ArchetypeDescription
direct_convergerFast, consistent improvement to very low final DeltaE (<=1.0) with few reversals.
slow_and_steadyMany actions, mostly improving, low oscillation; converges gradually.
coarse_then_fineDefault mixed strategy: rough adjustments followed by refinements.
oscillatorFrequent back-and-forth around the target (high gain-sign reversal rate).
random_searcherLow improvement rate with many revisits; exploration appears unfocused.
backsliderReached a better DeltaE earlier but ended notably worse than best-in-path.
short_runVery few actions (<3), insufficient trajectory length for richer patterning.
