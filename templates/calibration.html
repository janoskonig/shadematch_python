How sharp are your eyes today?

You'll see pairs of skin colours, one after another. For each pair you answer up to two quick questions: first “Are they identical?” — and if not, “Would that difference be acceptable on your face?” Some pairs are identical, some are obvious; most are subtle. There are no right answers and no feedback — just judge what you see.

It takes under 2 minutes. Best on a non-dimmed screen in even lighting. Do it across a few short sessions on different days — that's by design, and the picture of your eyes gets sharper each time.

Tip: you can also press 1 for yes, 2 for no.
