Build two palettes from the 327-pigment Kremer catalog (Hyperspectral Pigments, Zenodo 5592485) and compare their reachable colour gamut head-to-head. Gamut = CIELAB convex-hull volume of every Kubelka–Munk mixture, scored with the same engine the spectral mixer uses. For each palette, lock pigments with its A/B buttons in the catalog, then either Find widest gamut (a greedy search that adds the pigment most enlarging the gamut at each step) or Score picks to measure your exact set. The comparison below pits palette A against palette B — no fixed baseline.
